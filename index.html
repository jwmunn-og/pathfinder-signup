Not all who wander are lost.

Pathfinder is a planning platform for outdoor enthusiasts. With Pathfinder, you can build custom hikes based on an extensive nationwide trail database to plan your next adventure. No matter your destination, Pathfinder’s got you covered. Blending the reliability of the trusted map with the convenience and scope of today’s real-time data, Pathfinder enables you to search, build and share the hikes that are right for you, creating endless possibilities and streamlining the planning of your next outing.

Pathfinder is a social community. You can share and search hikes, collaborate on excursions, and post photos, ratings and reviews from the trail or from the comfort of your home.

Pathfinder tracks all of the details so that you don’t have to. From driving directions to weather conditions, Pathfinder provides updates to help you keep the details straight, making your next adventure the best yet.

Where technology meets trail.
